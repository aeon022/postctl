🚀 Meet postctl: Social Media Management Built for Developers & AI

Most social media scheduling tools are built for marketing teams. They require heavy web dashboards, clicky interfaces, and constant manual updates. 

postctl is different. It is a terminal-native CLI and TUI tool written in Go that treats your postings as code.

Key Features:
1️⃣ Markdown-first: Write your posts in your favorite IDE/editor (Vim/Neovim integrated!).
2️⃣ AI-as-Operator: Designed for AI terminal agents (like Claude/GPT) to run the full workflow. It uses non-interactive flags, dry-runs, and structured JSON output.
3️⃣ Bilingual (EN/DE): Fully internationalized docs, landing page, and assets.
4️⃣ Robust Headless Fallback: When Twitter/X rate-limits or blocks APIs, postctl silently spins up a headless Chrome instance to ensure your post gets published.
