## Tweet 2
Why is postctl perfect for AI?

1. No interactive prompts (controlled via flags)
2. `--format json` output for easy scripting
3. `--dry-run` to safely preview changes
4. Standardized exit codes (0/1/2/3)
5. Resume-on-failure for threads

Automation at its finest!

## Tweet 3
How it works:
1. AI scrapes your content/URL
2. AI drafts Markdown posts
3. AI runs `./postctl import [file]`
4. The local daemon publishes them
