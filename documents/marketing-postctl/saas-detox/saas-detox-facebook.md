Time for a SaaS Detox: Local-First Social Scheduling 🔒

If you're tired of paying monthly SaaS subscriptions just to queue posts, postctl offers a free, local-first alternative. 

By storing all credentials and drafts in a local SQLite database, postctl ensures absolute privacy and zero tracking. You can manage your posts directly from the terminal and export encrypted backups anytime.
