Social media drafting in Neovim. 💻

With postctl, you don't need browser dashboards. Press ctrl+v in the TUI to launch Vim/Neovim. Edit YAML frontmatter and drafts with live character rulers (max 500 for Mastodon), then save directly back to your local SQLite queue. 

Open source & terminal-focused:
👉 https://github.com/aeon022/postctl
