Still copying and pasting post text into a web browser? 💻

For terminal lovers, postctl lets you write and schedule posts without ever touching a mouse. Here is the Neovim editor flow. 👇
---
Built for keyboard-only speed in the TUI:
- Press `ctrl+v` in any field to suspend the TUI & open Neovim/Vim.
- Live character rulers at the top of your file showing platform limits (e.g. 280 for Twitter, 300 for Bluesky).
- Bi-directional YAML frontmatter sync on save.
---
Need to schedule? Press `ctrl+d` to launch a terminal calendar datepicker, select your day using arrows or hjkl, and hit enter to apply. No mouse required.
