Social media management as code: How AI runs postctl! 🤖

Instead of bloated SaaS tools, postctl is optimized for AI agents (like Claude or Antigravity) to act as operators. By generating Markdown posts with YAML frontmatter, the AI can validate, import, and schedule them programmatically using postctl's JSON-friendly CLI flags.
