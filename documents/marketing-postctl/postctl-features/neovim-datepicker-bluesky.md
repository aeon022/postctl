Draft your social posts in Neovim! 💻

Press ctrl+v in the postctl TUI to spawn Neovim with dynamic character rulers, write in Markdown, and sync back to SQLite on save. Use ctrl+d to open a terminal calendar datepicker.

Try it: https://postctl.sh
