Automate posts without cloud SaaS lock-in. 🔒

postctl's "AI-as-Operator" CLI design lets you run local AI scripts (using Claude or Ollama) to parse Markdown, validate character limits, and queue posts into your local SQLite database. Zero cloud tracking, zero telemetry.

100% open source and written in Go:
👉 https://github.com/aeon022/postctl
🌐 https://postctl.sh
