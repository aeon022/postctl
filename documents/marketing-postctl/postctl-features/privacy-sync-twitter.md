Why treat social media posts differently than code? 💻

postctl's "Social Media as Code" philosophy lets you write drafts in Markdown, version them in Git, and sync your scheduled queue across devices. Here is the workflow. 👇
---
How Multi-Device Sync works:

1. Write posts offline in Markdown.
2. Export your database as an encrypted binary (`postctl config export`).
3. Commit and push it to your private git repo.
4. Pull and import (`postctl config import`) on your VPS or other machines.
---
Safe, encrypted (AES-256-GCM), and completely free sync without cloud databases.
