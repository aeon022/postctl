A Markdown-first social media scheduler. 📝

postctl is a lightweight command-line tool that lets you manage your queue for Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Mastodon, Threads, Bluesky, and Facebook directly from your local terminal. 100% open-source, local-first, with zero cloud tracking.
