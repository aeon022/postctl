Keyboard-Only Workflow: Write Social Posts in Neovim 💻

Stop breaking your terminal focus to open web-based social schedulers. postctl allows you to draft your posts in your favorite terminal editor.

Pressing `ctrl+v` suspends the TUI and opens Neovim. The temporary file includes dynamic character rulers and a YAML frontmatter block that syncs directly back into your SQLite queue upon saving.
