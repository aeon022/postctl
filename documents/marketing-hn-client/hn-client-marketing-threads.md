🚀 Announcing hn-client: A gorgeous, keyboard-driven Hacker News client built for your terminal in Go!

Features:
• Vim navigation (j/k, Enter to view comments)
• Switch categories with Tab or 1-5
• Live search & filter with /
• Multi-color nested comments with HTML link sanitization
• Persistent read markings (✓) & [NEU] flags
• Suspends TUI to open pages in w3m/lynx

Get it on GitHub:
👉 https://github.com/aeon022/hn-client
