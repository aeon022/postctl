Keyboard-Driven Browsing: w3m/lynx Integration in hn-client 🖥️

Tired of context switching between your terminal and Chrome just to check a Hacker News link? 

hn-client solves this by suspending its Go/Bubble Tea loop and launching terminal-based web browsers like `w3m` directly inside the active window. Once you close the browser, your terminal state is restored instantly.

👉 https://github.com/aeon022/hn-client
