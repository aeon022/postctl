🚀 Meet hn-client — A modern, blazing-fast, keyboard-driven Hacker News TUI client for your terminal!

## Tweet 2

Keyboard-first design & Vim-style navigation:
• Navigate stories with `j`/`k` or arrows
• Instantly filter by title/author with `/`
• Switch feeds (Top, New, Best, Ask, Show) via `Tab` or `1-5` keys
• Open original links in w3m/lynx or your default browser with `o`

## Tweet 3

Color-coded nested comments:
• Complete reader mode directly in the terminal
• Beautiful indent guide colors
• Auto-parsed clean HTML links & code formatting
• Unread/read status (`✓`) & green `[NEU]` tags for fresh replies
