🚀 Say hello to postctl — a privacy-first, local-first social media scheduler for developers!

Tired of giving your session cookies and credentials to third-party web services?

postctl runs entirely on your local machine:
💾 Offline SQLite database
🔒 AES-256-GCM encrypted backup & multi-device sync
📝 Markdown parsing with Vim/Neovim edit flow
🖥️ Lightweight keyboard-driven terminal dashboard (TUI)

100% Open Source & built in Go.

Star or contribute on GitHub:
👉 https://github.com/aeon022/postctl
