PostCTL – TUI Social Media Schedules

If you like terminal, you probably hate bloated web UIs for managing your social media channels. Why not treat your posts like code?

Check out postctl: write in Markdown, edit in Vim, schedule locally.
---
With postctl, you can:
- Write posts in Markdown-first
- Edit in terminal with Neovim support
- Plan posts (calendar view & daemon scheduler)
- Sync encrypted database (AES-256-GCM)
- Post to X, LinkedIn, Threads, Mastodon & Bluesky!

Written in Go & SQLite.
---
Check out the code, run it locally, or contribute:
