🚀 Announcing postctl — Social Media Management, built for Developers!

Why use bloated SaaS web interfaces when you can schedule posts from your terminal?

postctl is a local-first CLI and TUI tool written in Go:
📝 Markdown-First: YAML metadata & Markdown body
⚙️ Vim/Neovim edit flow directly from terminal
🔒 Offline-First: sqlite storage with encrypted AES-256 multi-device sync
🐦 Posts to X, Bluesky, Mastodon, Threads & LinkedIn

Get started on GitHub:
👉 https://github.com/aeon022/postctl
