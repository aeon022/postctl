Tired of clicking through heavy web interfaces just to schedule a post? 

Say hello to postctl ⚡️

A terminal-native social media manager. Write posts in Markdown, import them to a local SQLite database, and schedule or publish them right from the command line.

And yes, it's designed so your local AI agent (like Claude/GPT) can schedule and publish posts for you.

Check it out: https://postctl.sh
