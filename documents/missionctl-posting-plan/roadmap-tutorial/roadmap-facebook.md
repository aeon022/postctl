🔮 The missionctl Vision: Our Roadmap for 2026/2027

When we started developing missionctl, our goal was clear: to build the "hands" of a local AI agent. By Q2 2027, you should be able to give Claude one sentence and have your entire digital week planned, scheduled, drafted, and tracked—all offline, in Go, with SQLite, and without opening a web browser.

Here is our launch timeline:
• Q3 2026: postctl v1.0 + MCP (social campaigns) & calctl v0.1 (macOS Calendar EventKit).
• Q4 2026: calctl v0.5 (Google Calendar integration), mailctl v0.1 (Apple Mail bridge), and budgetctl v0.1 (bank CSV).
• Q1 2027: Full TUI Mail client, budgetctl v0.5 rules, notectl v0.1 (Obsidian), and taskctl v0.1 (Reminders).
• Q2 2027: Full bundle integration with 43 MCP tools.
