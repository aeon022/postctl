💰 Meet budgetctl: Local-First, Privacy-Preserving Expense & Subscription Tracking.

Connecting your bank account to cloud apps exposes your financial history. budgetctl keeps transaction details on your disk, running entirely offline on SQLite.

Features:
• Import standard bank CSV files
• Custom regex classification rules
• Automatic subscription detection
• 9 MCP tools for Claude
