Manage your calendar and find free slots in your terminal. Meet calctl 📅

A lightweight Go binary bridging Apple Calendar via macOS EventKit.

• Sync Apple Calendar locally
• Keyboard monthly grid (Bubble Tea)
• Find free slots with `calctl free`
• 7 MCP tools

👉 https://github.com/aeon022/calctl
