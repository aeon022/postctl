Email from the terminal, built for AI agents. Meet `mailctl` 📧

A standalone Go binary that syncs your Apple Mail inbox locally, lets you search threads, and compose replies in Markdown. 🧵
---
Why `mailctl`?
- Local SQLite cache of inbox headers & text
- Suspend TUI to write drafts in Vim/Neovim
- Exposed as an MCP server with 6 tools (`inbox`, `search_email`, `send_email`, `draft_email`)
---
With mailctl wired to Claude Desktop, you can ask:
"Draft replies for my unread emails from today, flag urgent invoices, and add them to my tasks."

AI reads, writes drafts, and schedules reminders locally.
