💰 Introducing budgetctl: Local-First, Privacy-Preserving Expense & Subscription Tracking

Connecting bank accounts to cloud finance apps compromises your complete transaction history. budgetctl is a command-line budget manager written in Go that processes bank transactions entirely on your local machine.

Key Features:
• Local Imports: Import standard bank CSV exports (ING, N26, etc.) directly into a local SQLite database.
• Regex Classification: Create classification rules using regex patterns to auto-categorize transactions.
• Subscription Detection: Auto-detect recurring charges and unused SaaS subscriptions.
• MCP Integration: Exposes 9 tools to Claude to analyze budget details and suggest cuts locally.

👉 Check it out on GitHub: https://github.com/aeon022/budgetctl
