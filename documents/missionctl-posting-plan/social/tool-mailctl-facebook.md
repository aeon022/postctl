📧 Introducing mailctl: Email Management for the Monospace TUI & AI Era

Checking your inbox shouldn't require heavy webmails or syncing IMAP history to a cloud-based SaaS database. mailctl brings your email inbox directly into your local terminal workflow.

Key Features:
• macOS Bridge: Connects directly to Apple Mail using AppleScript to sync inbox headers locally.
• Markdown Drafting: Write emails in Vim/Neovim using YAML frontmatter (To, Subject) and Markdown.
• Model Context Protocol: Emits an MCP server exposing 6 tools to Claude (including inbox search, send, and draft).

👉 Build mailctl from source: https://github.com/aeon022/mailctl
