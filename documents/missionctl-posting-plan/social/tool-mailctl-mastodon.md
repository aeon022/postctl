📧 Meet mailctl: Email management for the monospace terminal.

Checking your inbox shouldn't mean cloud synchronizers reading your correspondence. mailctl caches your Apple Mail headers and bodies locally using a secure SQLite database.

Features:
• macOS AppleScript/Apple Mail bridge
• Edit drafts in Vim/Neovim with YAML headers
• Exposes 6 MCP tools for local AI agents

100% offline, private credentials.
👉 https://github.com/aeon022/mailctl
