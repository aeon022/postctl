Manage your calendar and find free slots directly in your terminal. Meet `calctl` 📅

A lightweight Go binary that bridges Apple Calendar via macOS EventKit. 🧵
---
Why `calctl`?
- Sync Apple Calendar locally
- View schedule in a keyboard-driven monthly grid (Bubble Tea)
- Find free gaps inside working hours instantly using `calctl free`
- Complete MCP server with 7 calendar tools
---
Wired to Claude Desktop, you can ask:
"Find a free 2-hour slot next Wednesday morning for deep work and schedule it as 'Refactoring'."

AI scans, finds the slot, and writes the event via macOS AppleScript/EventKit.
