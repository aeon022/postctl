🚀 Giving local AI agents "hands" via the command line!

Tired of cloud tools holding your data? missionctl is a local-first suite of 6 Go-based CLI/TUI tools that bridge your calendar, email, reminders, notes, budget, and social media locally. 

By exposing Model Context Protocol (MCP) servers, local AI agents like Claude Desktop can interact with your daily domains directly and securely via stdio.
