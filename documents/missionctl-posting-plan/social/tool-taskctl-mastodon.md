📝 Meet taskctl: Fast task management synced with Apple Reminders.

Most CLI task managers use custom text files that don't sync with mobile, or force you into a cloud subscription. 

taskctl combines a terminal CLI/TUI in Go with macOS Reminders sync (via Swift EventKit bridge). Best of all: it exposes 7 MCP tools, letting local AI agents manage your tasks.

100% local, SQLite backend.
👉 https://github.com/aeon022/taskctl
