Read, write, and index your Obsidian Markdown notes from the terminal. Meet `notectl` 📓

A lightweight Go binary that interacts with local markdown vaults and Apple Notes. 🧵
---
Why `notectl`?
- Sync & index local Obsidian directories
- Create or update notes using standard CLI inputs
- Automated Daily Note template generation
- Suspend TUI to write in Vim/Neovim
- Exposed as an MCP server with 7 tools (`read_note`, `write_note`, `search_notes`)
---
Wired to Claude Desktop, you can ask:
"Find all notes mentioning 'SaaS marketing' and append a summary of my latest ideas under the section 'Brainstorming'."

AI searches, parses headers, and writes directly into your local Markdown vault.
