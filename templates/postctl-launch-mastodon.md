🚀 I just built a terminal-native social media scheduler and publisher: postctl!

If you are a developer, you probably hate bloated web UIs for managing your social media channels. Why not treat your posts like code? 

With postctl, you can:
✍️ Write posts in Markdown-first files and version them in Git.
🖥️ Edit them inside an interactive, keyboard-driven Terminal UI (TUI) with Neovim support.
📅 Plan posts with a built-in calendar view and a daemon background scheduler.
🔒 Sync encrypted databases securely (AES-256) inside public repositories.
🎯 Post seamlessly to Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Threads, Mastodon, Bluesky, and Facebook!

It's completely written in Go, CGO-free, and powered by SQLite. 

Check out the code, run it locally, or contribute:
👉 https://github.com/aeon022/postctl
